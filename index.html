Common questions

Everything you're wondering

Will anyone know it was AI?
Probably not, and there's real evidence to back that up. BrightLocal ran a blind study where consumers rated AI-generated replies and owner-written replies side by side. They couldn't tell the difference, and when they couldn't, they actually preferred the AI replies. Reply AI is specifically engineered against the two signals AI detectors use: uniform sentence rhythm and predictable word choice. Every reply varies both deliberately.
Does it work on all Google review pages?
Yes, it works on Google Search (when you click Reply on your Business Profile from Search), Google Maps, and Google Business Profile (business.google.com). The extension detects whichever page you're on and injects the widget into the reply textarea automatically.
Does it actually post the reply for me?
Almost. Reply AI fills in Google's reply textarea with the generated text and activates the Post Reply button. You still click Post Reply yourself. This is intentional: you should read what's going up on your Business Profile before it goes live, especially on negative reviews. One click and it's done, but you're always in control of what gets posted.
What happens with 1-star reviews?
1-star and 2-star replies always get a "review before posting" flag in the reply card. They're never auto-posted. The prompt for these reviews follows a specific protocol: validate before apologising, name the specific failure, invite the conversation offline, and stay completely composed regardless of how hostile the original review was. These are the replies that matter most. Future customers read them.
Can I edit the reply before posting?
Yes, the generated reply appears in an editable card before it goes anywhere. You can edit it directly, hit "Different reply" to generate a new one, or copy it to the clipboard. Nothing goes into the Google textarea until you click "Post reply."
What are the 26 business types?
Restaurant/cafe, dental/medical clinic, HVAC/home services, retail store, hotel/hospitality, real estate agency, law firm, gym/fitness studio, auto repair shop, hair/beauty salon, accounting/finance, veterinary clinic, cleaning service, landscaping/lawn care, childcare/daycare, photography/videography, pet grooming, moving company, plumbing/electrical, spa/wellness centre, tutoring/education, chiropractor/physiotherapy, insurance agency, coffee shop/specialty cafe, e-commerce/online store, and general local business as the catch-all. Each has its own prompt guidance. A dental reply never reads like an auto shop reply.
Is my review data private?
Review text is sent to our backend (hosted on Railway) and to Anthropic's API to generate the reply. It is not stored permanently, sold, or used for training. Your settings (business name, tone, brand voice) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. They never leave your device except as part of the generate request. See our privacy policy for full details.
