Get started in 3 steps

1

Set up your business

Open the Reply AI popup (click the extension icon in Chrome's toolbar). Select your business type, enter your business name, and choose a default tone. Takes a minute and makes a real difference to reply quality.

Pro tip: add a brand voice. Describe how you write, or paste a reply you love. The AI will match your style exactly.

Open settings
2

Go to your Google Business Profile

Navigate to business.google.com and open the Reviews section. Find a review that needs a response and click Reply.

The Reply AI widget will appear automatically below the reply box. You don't need to do anything else.

Open Google Business
3

Click "Generate reply"

The widget shows a preview of the review, the age of the review, and a big "Generate reply" button. Click it and a reply appears in the text box within 2–3 seconds, ready to post.

Not happy with the result? Click Different reply for a completely different version. Want something tweaked? Edit directly in the box before posting.
